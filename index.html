Guys Having Coffee

Men's Group in Lakeland, FL

Life gets busy, and genuine connection can be hard to find.

Hey, I'm Dan. I started Guys Having Coffee because I was looking for a low-pressure way to connect with other men in the community—and figured I wasn't the only one.

This is a space where men can slow down, grab a cup of coffee, and just be themselves. No RSVP required. No agenda. No pressure. Show up when you can, stay as long as you'd like, and share as much or as little as you want.

Whether you're looking for meaningful conversation, a few laughs, or simply a welcoming place to connect with other men, you're welcome here. Sign up to stay in the loop, or reach out if you have questions.
